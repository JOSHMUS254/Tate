History of Akan Names

The Akan people are an ethnic group primarily from Ghana and parts of Ivory Coast. Akan names are deeply tied to their culture, beliefs, and traditions.

Origin and Meaning Akan names are traditionally based on the day of the week a person is born. This system of “day names” is rooted in the Akan belief that the day of birth influences personality and destiny. Each day of the week has a male and female name.
